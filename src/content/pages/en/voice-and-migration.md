This subproject examines the role of spoken performance and multilingual writing in the works of Jewish women who crossed linguistic and geographic borders.

### Authors in Focus

- Leah Goldberg
- Mascha Kaleko
- Else Lasker-Schuler
- Jacqueline Kahanov

### Research Questions

- How does performed voice reshape literary meaning?
- How do oral and written forms interact in migration contexts?
- What new forms of Jewish cultural belonging emerge through multilingual expression?
