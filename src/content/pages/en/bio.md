## Academic Profile

Dr. Michal Peles-Almagor is a scholar of comparative literature and Jewish cultural history. Her work focuses on Hebrew and German literature, Jewish literary and cultural history, modernism, migration studies, and theater and voice studies.

## Current Project

*Voice and Migration in Jewish Women's Writing* investigates the role of performed voice and multilingual expression in the literary works of Jewish women writers who experienced migration and dislocation. The project focuses on Leah Goldberg, Mascha Kaleko, Else Lasker-Schuler, and Jacqueline Kahanov, and examines how recorded performance and written text together reshape Jewish public oral spheres.
