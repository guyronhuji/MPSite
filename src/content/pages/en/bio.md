## Academic Profile

Dr. Michal Peles-Almagor works at the intersection of comparative literature, Jewish cultural history, and modernism, with special attention to multilingual writing, migration, and the relationship between spoken and written traditions.

## Current Appointment

- Martin Buber Society of Fellows Postdoctoral Fellow, The Hebrew University of Jerusalem (2024-present)
