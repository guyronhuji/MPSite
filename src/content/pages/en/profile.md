Dr. Michal Peles-Almagor is a scholar of comparative literature and Jewish cultural history, with a focus on Hebrew and German literature, migration, multilingualism, and literary voice.

She is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Martin Buber Society of Fellows at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem.

Her research and teaching combine literary analysis with cultural history and performance studies, with special attention to how texts move across languages, places, and traditions.
