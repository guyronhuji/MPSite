## Current Project

### Voice and Migration in Jewish Women's Writing

This project studies how performed voice and multilingual expression operate in the writing of Jewish women who experienced migration and dislocation. It focuses on Leah Goldberg, Mascha Kaleko, Else Lasker-Schuler, and Jacqueline Kahanov.

By reading recorded performances together with literary texts, the project traces how oral and written forms interact across language, geography, and cultural belonging.
